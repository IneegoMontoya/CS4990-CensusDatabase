This folder has all of the raw data sets before they were trimmed down for the purposes of this database. 

The Missouri_COUNTY csv was acquired from Data.gov and used to acquire the names, geographic area, and population numbers for 2010. It can be found at

https://svi.cdc.gov/Documents/Data/2016_SVI_Data/CSV/States_Counties/Missouri_COUNTY.csv

In order to acquire the data for other states, simply replace the Missouri near the end of the URL with your desired state name.
